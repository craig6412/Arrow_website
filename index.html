About the Game

Core Loop

Fly through rings to score. Keep your streak alive to build massive combos. Miss a ring or collide with obstacles and it’s game over—one more try!

  • Green rings = standard points
  • Blue rings = combo/bonus chains
  • Bonus rings = big score boosts

Control Styles

  • Tap/Hold: Tap to rise, release to fall (classic).
  • Thumb-Drag: Press anywhere; slide up/down for smooth control.
  • Tilt (Landscape): Steer like a wheel while gravity does the rest.

Dev Option: Adjust the speed-drop multiplier to simulate real-world drag and get the exact “feel” you like.
